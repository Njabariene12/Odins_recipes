Beef Stew recipe

Description

This easy beef stew follows a straightforward prep and finishes with that classic old fashioned flavor

It is made with tender bite size pieces of beef, creamy potatoes, nutritious carrots, and a rich and savory red wine infused broth.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 lbs. chuck roast, trimmed of excess fa
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 1/2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups chopped yellow onion
  • 1.5 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 1/2 cups beef stock
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 Tbsp fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 20 oz. red potatoes, scrubbed and rinsed clean
